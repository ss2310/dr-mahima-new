8 Criteria for Choosing the Best Dentist in Kota

Before we talk about any specific clinic, here's what actually separates a good dentist from an average one. Use this as your checklist when evaluating any dental practice in Kota.

1

Qualifications & Registration

Minimum BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery). For specialized treatments, look for MDS. Verify registration with the Rajasthan Dental Council — it's a red flag if the dentist can't show their registration number.

Dr. Shashikant: BDS (2006), MDS (2025), Reg. A-2737
2

Sterilization Protocols

The gold standard is a Class-B autoclave for instrument sterilization. Single-use items (needles, gloves, cotton) should never be reused. Ask directly: "Can I see your autoclave?" — a confident clinic will happily show you.

Class-B autoclave + 4-step sterilization protocol
3

Transparent Pricing

A trustworthy clinic publishes its prices or provides written estimates before treatment. If pricing is vague or changes after procedures begin, walk away. Hidden charges are the #1 patient complaint in dental care.

Every price published online. Written estimates always.
4

Years of Clinical Experience

Experience matters — especially for complex procedures like implants, RCT, and orthodontics. A clinic with 10+ years of active practice has seen enough cases to handle unusual situations confidently.

13+ years serving families in Kota
5

Google Reviews & Patient Feedback

Real, detailed reviews on Google from verified patients are more reliable than testimonials on the clinic's own website. Look for consistency in feedback — not just the star rating but what patients actually say.

4.9/5 average on Google with 100+ reviews
6

Equipment & Technology

Look for: digital X-ray (RVG) — lower radiation, faster diagnosis; rotary endodontics for painless single-sitting RCT; intraoral camera; and a hygienic, well-maintained clinic environment.

Digital RVG, rotary endo, modern equipment
7

Communication & Consent

A good dentist explains what they're doing, shows you the X-ray or problem area, discusses alternatives, and gets clear consent before starting. If a clinic rushes you or pushes upsells, reconsider.

Every procedure explained before it begins
8

Accessibility & Availability

Can you get an appointment when you need one? Are emergency slots available? Are Sunday hours offered for working professionals? A clinic with limited availability is a clinic you'll struggle with long-term.

Our Treatment Protocols — What Every Visit Looks Like

Transparency starts with knowing what to expect. Here's exactly how we handle every patient visit, from consultation to follow-up.

1

Initial Consultation (₹200)

You'll meet the doctor, discuss your concerns, get a thorough oral examination, and digital X-rays if needed. We explain what we see and what options exist — no treatment starts here.

2

Written Treatment Plan & Cost Estimate

Before anything begins, you receive a written plan with every step and every cost. We discuss alternatives, risks, and timelines. You decide whether to proceed — with no pressure.

3

Pre-Treatment Sterilization

Every instrument used in your treatment goes through a 4-step sterilization protocol ending in a Class-B autoclave. We use fresh disposables (gloves, needles, cotton) for every patient.

4

Painless Anesthesia Application

Before any injection, we apply topical numbing gel so you don't feel the needle. Advanced techniques ensure the treatment area is fully numb before we begin.

5

Treatment with Continuous Communication

We explain what's happening as we work. If discomfort arises, signal us — we pause and adjust. For longer procedures, we take breaks and check in regularly.

6

Post-Treatment Care Instructions

Written and verbal instructions for recovery — what to eat, how to care for the area, warning signs to watch for, and our number for any emergency follow-up. Free follow-up visits for the first month.
