How Much Do Dental Implants Cost in Kota?

The cost of a single dental implant in Kota ranges from ₹15,000 to ₹50,000 per tooth in 2026. This price typically includes the titanium implant fixture, the abutment connector, and the ceramic crown.

At Dr. Mahima's Dental Care in Talwandi, we offer transparent packages starting at ₹15,000 for budget-friendly options and up to ₹50,000 for premium international brands. Most treatments are completed in 3–6 months with a success rate of over 95%.

Why Does the Price Vary? (₹15k vs ₹50k)

Not all implants are the same. The variation depends on three main choices:

1. The Implant Brand

Budget (₹15,000 – ₹22,000): Standard FDA-approved implants (e.g., Israeli or Korean brands). Excellent for single back teeth.

Premium (from ₹50,000): Premium International Brands. Recommended for front teeth or complex cases due to faster healing.

2. The Crown Material

PFM (Porcelain Fused to Metal): Strong, affordable, but can show a thin metal line over time.

Zirconia (Metal-Free): The gold standard. 100% natural look, extremely durable, and biocompatible.

3. Additional Procedures (If Needed)

Bone Grafting: If your jawbone is thin, we may need to build it up (Cost: ₹5,000 – ₹10,000).

Sinus Lift: Required only for upper jaw cases with low bone height.

Your Journey to a New Smile (3–6 Months)

Step 1

The Analysis (Day 1)

We perform a 3D Scan (CBCT) to check your bone density. Dr. Mahima creates a digital surgical plan.

Step 2

The Placement (Day 2–3)

The titanium implant is placed into the jawbone. This is a painless procedure performed under local anesthesia. It takes 45–60 minutes per tooth.

Step 3

The Healing (3 Months)

We let the implant bond with your bone (Osseointegration). You wear a temporary tooth if needed, so you never go toothless.

Step 4

The Final Tooth (Month 3–4)

Once bonded, we attach the permanent Zirconia or Ceramic crown. You are ready to eat, smile, and live normally!
