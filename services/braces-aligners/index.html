Your Options Explained

Metal Braces — ₹15,000 – ₹55,000

The most affordable and durable option. Best for complex cases, severe misalignment, crowding, and bite issues. Popular with students and budget-conscious patients.

Ceramic Braces — ₹25,000 – ₹80,000

Tooth-colored brackets that blend with your natural teeth. Same effectiveness as metal braces but far more discreet — ideal for working professionals.

Self-Ligating Braces — ₹50,000 – ₹1.1 Lakh

Use specialized clips instead of elastics, often reducing treatment time. Require fewer adjustments and are easier to clean.

Clear Aligners — ₹50,000 – ₹3 Lakh

Removable, invisible, and comfortable. Premium brands like Invisalign at the higher end, while Indian brands like Toothsi start around ₹50,000–₹90,000.

Your Treatment Timeline

Month 0

Consultation & Scans

Digital scans, X-rays, and a personalized treatment plan. We discuss your options and costs upfront.

Month 1

Braces Placement

Brackets and wires are bonded to your teeth. Completely painless, takes about 60–90 minutes.

Monthly

20-Min Adjustments

Quick monthly visits to tighten wires or switch to the next set of aligners.

Month 18–24

Removal & Retainers

Braces are removed and you receive retainers to maintain your new smile.
