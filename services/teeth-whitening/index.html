Why Are Teeth Yellow? Common Causes

☕ Coffee & Tea

Tannins in chai and coffee stick to enamel over time.

🚬 Smoking/Tobacco

Brown, stubborn tar stains that brushing can't remove.

⏰ Aging

Enamel thins with age, revealing the yellow inner dentin layer.

🌿 Haldi & Diet

Indian diets rich in turmeric and curry are the #1 cause of yellowing.

The 45-Minute Process

Step 1

Polishing

Removing surface stains and plaque buildup with professional-grade polish.

Step 2

Protection

A protective blue resin is painted over your gums to ensure the strong gel never touches your soft tissues.

Step 3

Application

The whitening gel is carefully brushed onto the 'Smile Zone' — the front 8–10 teeth.

Step 4

Activation

We shine a specialized LED cool light on the teeth for 15–20 minutes. This activates the gel. (Repeated 2–3 times).

Step 5

The Reveal

We wash off the gel and hand you a mirror. Most patients say 'Wow!' immediately.
