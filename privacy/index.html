Dr. Mahima's Dental Care ("we", "us", or "our") operates the website drmahimasdentalcare.in and associated advertising forms. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and protection of personal information when you use our services.

Information We Collect

When you book an appointment through our website, Google Ads lead form, or WhatsApp, we may collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Phone number
  • Treatment interest or dental concern
  • Preferred appointment time
  • Any additional information you voluntarily share (e.g. medical conditions relevant to treatment)

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information solely for the following purposes:

  • To confirm and schedule your dental appointment

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your information is only shared with the dental team at Dr. Mahima's Dental Care for the purpose of providing you with treatment and care.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Google Ads & Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics and Google Ads to understand how visitors use our site and to measure advertising effectiveness. These services may collect anonymized data such as your browser type, device, pages visited, and general location. This data is governed by Google's privacy policy and does not include your name, phone number, or personal details.

WhatsApp Communication

When you contact us via WhatsApp, your messages and phone number are processed through WhatsApp's platform. WhatsApp's own privacy policy applies to data transmitted through their service. We only use your WhatsApp information to communicate about your dental appointment and care.
